About Me

Peter Mora IV is a second-year student at Brown University concentrating in Computer Science. Currently, he is fluent in programming in Java, Python, and C in addition to possessing reverse engineering skills through reading assembly. Mr. Mora is particularly interested in Cyber Security, and as a member of Air Force ROTC, he aims to commission as a Cyber Warfare Officer in either the Air Force or Space Force upon graduation. He presently serves as a PT Planning Deputy Commander and Professional Development Team Lead for the i5space program at AFROTC Detachment 340. He is also interested in leveraging AI and Machine Learning, and understanding its implications towards Cyber Security in the future. Mr. Mora also served as Vice-Chairman, Parliamentarian, and Treasurer of the Stephen Hopkins Society at Brown, is a Lector and Bible Study member in the Brown-RISD Catholic Community, and represents Brown in the Collegiate Chess League.
