About Me

Peter Mora IV is a second-year student at Brown University concentrating in Computer Science and Economics. Currently, he is fluent in programming in Java and Python. Mr. Mora is particularly interested in Cyber Security, and as a member of Air Force ROTC, he aims to commission as a Cyber Warfare Officer in either the Air Force or Space Force upon graduation. He is also interested in leveraging AI and Machine Learning, and understanding its implications towards Cyber Security in the future. Mr. Mora also served as Vice-Chairman, Parliamentarian, and Treasurer of the Stephen Hopkins Society, is a Lector and Bible Study member in the Brown-RISD Catholic Community, and represents Brown in the Collegiate Chess League.
